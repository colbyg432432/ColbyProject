Who Am I?

As Colby Geary, my journey with golf started back in the eighth grade, a passion that has only grown stronger over the years. Playing high school golf was a pivotal experience, laying the foundation for my deep understanding of the game and honing my skills on the course. Now, with each swing, each round, I'm constantly striving to improve, always seeking that perfect balance of skill, strategy, and mental focus that golf demands. My dedication to the sport is unwavering, whether it's practicing long drives, refining my short game, or studying the nuances of golf swings. Through my platform, Golfgab, I aim to share this passion and my journey of continual improvement with fellow enthusiasts, offering insights, tips, and engaging in the rich, ever-evolving world of golf.

Why Did I Start GolfGab?

I started GolfGab driven by a belief in the transformative power of community and data in the game of golf. My firsthand experience in golf taught me that improvement comes not just from practice, but from understanding and analyzing one's game. This led to the creation of the stats tracking page, a tool I envisioned could significantly impact a golfer's performance by providing detailed insights into various aspects of their play. Additionally, I recognized the value of shared knowledge and experiences, which is why I included a forums section. This platform allows golfers to connect, discuss strategies, share experiences, and offer advice. I firmly believe that by using Golfgab's stats tracker and engaging in thoughtful discussions with fellow golfers, players at all levels can achieve substantial improvements in their game. It's more than just a website; it's a community dedicated to elevating every member's love and skill for golf.
